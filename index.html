    Welcome to SignalRTC

    This is a browser-based video chat application powered by WebRTC for live media and SignalR for text chat and signaling.

    For the best experience, use Chrome or Firefox and allow your browser to access your camera and microphone when prompted.

    After you press Start, you can see other visitors, send public or private messages, and choose someone for a direct audio or video call.

    You can switch on your webcam or microphone from the device panel, then use Call selected user to begin a conversation.

    Messages are not saved and the experience is still experimental, so feedback is always welcome.
